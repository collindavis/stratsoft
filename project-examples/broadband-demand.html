Broadband Demand

Back to Project Examples

Data from a well known and respected data seller recorded some 55,000 eligible businesses in a project study region. By combining this with other data sources the resultant Stratsoft data-set contained over 88,000 business prospect records.

The Challenge

Understanding the true scope of broadband demand in a given market requires going beyond any single data source. Standard commercial datasets often under-count eligible businesses — missing newly formed companies, businesses operating from non-traditional locations, or firms classified under unexpected industry codes.

The Stratsoft Approach

By systematically combining multiple data sources and applying proprietary data conditioning and deduplication algorithms, Stratsoft was able to expand the initial dataset of 55,000 records to over 88,000 verified business prospect records — a 60% increase in addressable market visibility.

Each record was geocoded and linked to building-level infrastructure data, enabling precise identification of which prospects could be served by existing network assets and which required incremental investment.

Outcomes

  • 88,000+ business prospect records — a 60% increase over the commercial baseline
  • Geocoded database linked to existing fiber network infrastructure
  • Segmented demand analysis by addressability, bandwidth tier, and payback period
  • Transferable decision-support tool for ongoing sales and network planning use
